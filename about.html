  • LEADERSHIP 2014-2015



In 2013, the Dalton Model United Nations Team hosted its first conference, DMUN I, with over 200 delegates and 10 committees. Led by Directors-General Aiden Slavin and Harriet Fisher, DMUN became an integral part of the conference circuit on the Upper East Side.

The following year, DMUN II was another resouding success. Directors-General Abigail Kasdin and David Waltcher expanded the conference to over 400 delegates in 9 committees, including a simulation of the 2016 White House Situation Room by current Secretaries-General Philip Clark and Andrew Milich.

This year, Directors-General Jordan Singer and Beatrice Edmonds have begun to plan DMUN III, a larger and more exclusive experience for nearby schools. With 8 committees and a hearty staff, we look forward to welcoming delegates to The Dalton School in April!
